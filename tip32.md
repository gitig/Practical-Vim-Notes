#Tip32: Tab-Complete Your Ex Commands  
  
##:col&lt;C-d&gt;  
>reveal a list of possible completions  
>`<Tab>` to cycle through the list  
>`<S-Tab>` to scroll backward  
  
![tip32_1](images/tip32_1.png)  
      
  
##scroll forward  
>`<Tab>`, `<C-n>`, `<Right>`  
  
##scroll backward  
>`<S-Tab>`, `<C-p>`, `<Left>`
