# Tip1: Meet the Dot Command

## Dot:
>repeat the last change.

## x:
>delete the character under the cursor.

## u:
>undo the changes.

## dd:
>delete current line as a whole.

## >G:
>increase the indentation from the current line until the end of the file.

## h,j,k,l:
>h:left
>j:up
>k:down
>l:right

## i:
>enter Insert mode

## Esc:
>enter Normal mode
